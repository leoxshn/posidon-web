posidon launcher

Minimal, feature-rich, different.

Posidon launcher is a minimal, one page homescreen with a vertical scrolling feed. It was designed to work well on big phones, so it's easy to reach most things on the screen.


Features

- News & Notifications feed
- Wallpaper gallery
- Experimental desktop mode for Android 10
- Vector and animated iconpack support
- Blur behind app drawer
- 5 Font options
- App search
- A lot more
